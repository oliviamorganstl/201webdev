What came first True Love

Once upon a time I had a roommate who loved Dino Nuggets. She dipped them in ranch dressing, which was disgusting, but she would occasionally give me a nugget. This is where my love of dino nugs originated.

What came second Taylor Wells

Taylor Wells asked the class what he should make the website about. I knew that I needed to submit Dino Nuggets on the Google Form. It was a random thought, but I knew that people could love the idea. So I submitted the form over and over and over again until Taylor Wells decided to put my idea to the test.

What came last The class spoke

While the first Google Form was clearly rigged, thanks to me and my boyfriend entering Dino Nuggets repeatedly, the second survey was an overwhelming success. In the words of a guy who sat behind me, "I wanted a NFL website, but dino nuggets is winning by so much I guess I will just do that." Democracy at its finest right there.
